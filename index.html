About

Design is about
connecting with people.

"I don't limit myself to a single style — I love exploring different aesthetics to craft visuals that resonate."

I'm a graphic designer with over five years of experience in branding, social media design, layout design, creative ads, presentations, and packaging. Having worked as a freelancer, in an agency, and in-house, I bring a well-rounded perspective to every project.

I craft visuals that align with brand identities and engage audiences — whether that's a full identity system, a social campaign for an automotive brand, or packaging for a food delivery app.
