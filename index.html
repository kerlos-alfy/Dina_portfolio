Dina AssemSenior Graphic Designer · Cairo

Design that adapts without losing direction.

Every brand needs a different visual language. The goal is to find that language, make it feel premium, and keep it consistent across every touchpoint.

Who I Am

Brand-first thinker.

Dina approaches every project by understanding the audience, the tone, and the feeling the brand needs to leave behind.

What I Create

Campaign-ready visuals.

Identity, packaging, social posts, ads, layouts, and presentations are crafted to look strong on their own and connected as a system.

How I Think

Taste with structure.

The work starts with a concept, then becomes a clear visual direction through hierarchy, composition, color, typography, and detail.

What I Bring

Polish that ships.

Creative exploration is matched with organized files, fast adaptation, and production-ready execution for real campaigns and real teams.

Creative Focus

A flexible skillset around one sharp creative core.

The portfolio spans many industries and aesthetics, but the core stays the same: clear hierarchy, strong composition, consistent systems, and visuals that fit the brand instead of following a template.
